# Bulgur and Cashew Stuffed Eggplant

## Ingredients

- *1 tbsp* olive oil
- *3 cloves* garlic (crushed)
- *0.5 c* golden raisins
- *0.5 tsp* curry powder
- *0.25 tsp* salt
- *1 c* quick-cooking bulgur
- *2 c* water
- *2* eggplants (medium)
- *2 tbsp* olive oil
- *0.5 tsp* salt
- *0.5 c* cashews
- mint (chopped)

## Cookware

- saucepot
- baking sheet

## Steps

### Bulgur Mixture

1. In small saucepot, heat olive oil on medium. Add garlic, golden raisins,
curry powder, and salt. Cook 2 minutes, stirring. Add quick-cooking bulgur and
water. Heat to simmering. Cover; simmer 15 minutes, or until bulgur is tender.

### Eggplant Preparation

1. Meanwhile, cut eggplants in half lengthwise. Scoop out seeds. Arrange
eggplant on baking sheet lined with foil, cut sides up. Brush with olive oil
and sprinkle with salt. Broil on high (6 inches from heat source) 7 minutes, or
until tender.

### Assembly

1. Remove eggplant from oven; cover with foil. With fork, fluff bulgur; stir in
cashews. Stuff eggplant with bulgur mixture; garnish with mint.
