# Belgian waffles

## Ingredients

- non stick cooking spray
- *2.25 c* all purpose flour
- *1 Tbsp* baking powder
- *3 Tbsp* sugar
- *0.5 tsp* salt
- *1 tsp* cinnamon
- *2* egg whites
- *2* egg yolks
- *0.5 c* vegetable oil
- *1 c* milk
- *1 tsp* vanilla extract

## Cookware

- waffle iron
- large bowl
- medium bowl
- hand mixer
- medium bowl

## Steps

1. Preheat your waffle iron, spray with non stick cooking spray and set aside.

2. In a large bowl whisk together the all purpose flour, baking powder, sugar,
salt, and cinnamon.

3. In a medium bowl beat the egg whites with a hand mixer until stiff peaks
form. Set aside.

4. In a medium bowl mix together the egg yolks, vegetable oil, milk, and vanilla
extract.

5. Add the egg yolk mixture to the dry ingredients and mix well.

6. Fold in the egg whites.

7. Pour 2/3c of  batter onto the hot waffle iron and cook until golden brown.

8. Serve immediately with butter, syrup, powdered sugar or any other favorite
toppings.
