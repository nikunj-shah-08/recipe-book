# Dutch baby

## Ingredients

- *3* eggs
- *0.5 cup* flour
- *0.5 cup* milk
- *1 tbsp* sugar
- *0.125 tsp* nutmeg
- *2 tbsp* butter

## Cookware

- 10-inch cast iron skillet

## Steps

### Batter

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees

2. Whisk together eggs, flour, milk, sugar, nutmeg. Allow to hydrate for 10
minutes.

3. Add butter to 10-inch cast iron skillet and place in oven. Melt butter until
it starts to bubble.

4. Whisk batter for 1-2 minutes and add to pan. Bake for 16 minutes.

5. Reduce oven to 300 degrees and bake for an additional 5 minutes.
