# Zucchini Carrot Apple Muffins

## Ingredients

- *0.75 cup* zucchini (shredded, excess water removed)
- *0.5 cup* apple (shredded, excess water removed)
- *0.5 cup* carrot (shredded, excess water removed)
- *4 Tbsp* butter (melted)
- *0.5 cup* sugar
- *0.25 cup* applesauce
- *2* eggs
- *1 tsp* vanilla
- *1.5 cups* flour (white whole wheat)
- *1 tsp* baking soda
- *1 tsp* cinnamon
- *0.5 tsp* ?ginger (optional)

## Cookware

- bowl
- muffin tins

## Steps

1. Mix zucchini, apple, carrot, butter, sugar, applesauce, eggs, and vanilla in
a large bowl and stir until well combined.

2. Add flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and ?ginger and stir until just mixed.
Batter will be very thick. Stir well to ensure all flour is mixed in.

3. Scoop into greased or lined muffin tins.

4. Bake at 375 degrees F for 20 minutes or until toothpick inserted into center
comes out clean.
